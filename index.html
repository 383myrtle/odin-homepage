Qin Shi Huang

About me

Visionary leader and unifier of China with extensive experience in empire-building, administrative reform, and large-scale infrastructure projects. Established the first centralized imperial government, standardized laws, currency, and writing systems, and spearheaded the construction of the Great Wall. Adept at strategic military campaigns, bureaucratic restructuring, and long-term political consolidation.
